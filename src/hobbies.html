Activities I Enjoy the Most in My Free Time

Create|Play|Watch|Taste – Explore the Best of Every World!

Movies

"Cinematic Escapes: My Passion for Movies"

Ever had the need to take a moment to stop and change your prespective? For me, movies satisfy that urge. I like to lose myself in other worlds. Whether they are entertaining tales or amusing adventures, they frequently inspire me with new concepts.I'm constantly struck by how characters persevere, support one another, and find hope even under the most trying circumstances. It is amazing how a great movie can change your outlook on life.At the end, it is during those silent moments following the film's ends that I come to appreciate how a those stories can change my viewpoint at the very moment I need it most.

Music

"My Life's Soundtrack: My Love of Music"

Whenever I need a mood boost or a respite from the daily grind, I turn to music. You'll rarely find me without my AirPods, whether I'm heading to class, going for a walk, or simply getting my day started. Ethiopian oldies musics hold a unique place in my heart because they embody an unsaid depth that I can't fully articulate. It's the type of music that transcends time, combining exquisite melodies with moving lyrics. It's not just about listening them ; it's about feeling every note , letting the rhythm guides you . It's a reminder that no matter where life takes me, the rhythm of the past will always guide my heart home.

Reading

"Flipping Pages: An Exploration of Literature and Creativity"

Books have always given me a comfort zone, specially funny, light-hearted comedy books. But I also enjoy delving into books about Ethiopian history and I find it fascinating that knowing the past gives you a new perspective on the present and future.As the saying goes, 'without a past there is no future', and these books enrich my outlook on my history. Along the way, I have discovered some authors whose work really speaks to me. One of my favorites is Author Alex Abreham.I always look forward to his work.I've read almost all of his books, and each one gives me new ideas and a greater appreciation for storytelling.

Volleyball-Game

"Game On: My Love of Volleyball and the Team Spirit"

I have a fondness for vollyball began in high school, and since then, it has been my passion. It goes beyond the game —it’s about hanging out with friends, feeling the rush of competition, and simply having fun. I've practiced and worked to get better, and I want to keep pushing myself further. It gives me that sense of release, and moment of focus, and I find both mentally and physically fulfilling. Beyond just a sport, it’s a way to connect with other , push myself, and savour the small things in life. I'm looking forward to continuing this journey and deepen my passion for the game.

Video-Editing

"Innovative Creativity: The Craft of Video Editing"

I initially enjoyed video editing as a pastime, but it soon turned into something I really love. I enjoy assembling clips, determining appropriate transitions, and observing the final product. Regardless of the length, editing allows me to experiment with various sounds and images. I want to watch how a basic concept may be transformed into something visually striking masterpiece.
